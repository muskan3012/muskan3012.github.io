Hi, I'm Muskan Vashistha

My artistic journey began as child, where I spent hours drawing and creating. I love experimenting with different materials and techniques, from watercolor and gouache to fineliners and canvas. When I'm not painting you can find me hanging out with my friends and exploring different places to eat. For years, I was obsessed with getting everything just right, but now I've realise that sometimes good is just good enough.

WHAT I MAKE

Mandala

It represents the universe and its transformation from that of suffering into one of joy. It is an expression of healing and goodwill.

Abstract Line Art

Line, to an average person, has absolutely no meaning. But to an Artist, it is used to create magnificent workof art full of rhythm and emotions.

Doodle

Doodling gives you an emotional outlet and helps calm you down. Even if you doodle absent-mindedly doesn't mean your mind is absent.

Painting

Painting is a therapeutic and enjoyable way to relax the mind and reduce stress. It is a wonderful approach to discover inner quiet and peace.
